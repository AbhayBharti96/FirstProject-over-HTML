Maggi Recipie

  1. Prepare water
  2. Pour the Maggie inside the Pot
  3. Stir all that one until the threads are looking seperated.
  4. After Complete boil add masala to Maggie.
  5. Now it is ready for serving.

INGREDIENTS FOR MAGGI

  • Onion Powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Garlic Powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Coriander Powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Chilli Powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric Powder - ½ tsp
  • Cumin Powder - 1 tsp
  • Pepper Powder - 1 tsp
  • Fenugreek Powder - ¼ tsp
  • Ginger Powder - ½ tsp
  • Garam Masala Powder - 1.5 tsp
  • Sugar - 1 tblspn
  • Red Chilli Flakes - 2 tsp
  • Salt - 1.5 tsp
  • Cornflour - 1 tsp
  • Citric Acid - ½ tsp
  • Amchoor Powder - ½ tsp
  • Tomato Powder or Tomato Soup Powder - 1.5 tbsp
